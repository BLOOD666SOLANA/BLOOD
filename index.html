HOW TO BUY

Download & set up your wallet of choice. (We recommend Phantom Wallet). You can download the wallet as a browser extension or as an app on you phone at no cost. Please ensure that you save your secret recovery phrase in a secure location after creating your wallet & do not share it with anybody.
If your wallet does not yet have Eth (Ethereum) you can purchase it directly from Metamask or transfer it from a centralized exchange (Coinbase, Kraken etc) and withdraw it to your wallet.
Go to https://raydium.io/swap (Metamask features an integrated browser) to connect your wallet. Paste in the $BLOOD contract address into the field that you want to exchange/swap your SOL for.
Choose the amount of SOL you want to exchange for $BLOOD and click “Swap”. You will have to follow the prompts in your wallet to confirm the swap action. Congratulations! You have now given your wallet a transfusion of $Blood.
